Guild Rules

By joining the guild you have agreed to the rules below. Breaking the rules will risk in being removed from the guild. New rules may be added by Flora’s discretion.

1

Respect all members and people outside of the guild

If there are complaints with proof(screenshots, recordings, etc.) you'll be kicked.

2

Actively Level

This can mean being on everyday, a couple times a week, or weekends only.

3

No spam smegaing or chain

Using super megaphones multiple times in a row with a short timeframe is not allowed.

4

Don't do anything bannable

No hacking/botting/ scamming/harassing/vote abusing, etc. Find full list on MapleLegend rules.

5

Refrain from using inappropriate language in guild/alliance chat

This includes the "n" word, using mental health disorders as a degrading term, and death jokes.

6

Impersonating or claiming to be someone you are not

Pretty straight forward.

7

Let us know when you're going to be inactive

Let Flora or a Jr Leader know if you plan to be inactive for a period of time due to school or work to avoid being kicked
