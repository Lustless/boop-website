Join Us!

We are recruiting! Currently, we are accepting new members in our guilds Boop and Booper. Sadly, yes, we have some standards, so they're requirements :p Nothing to worry about, though! They aren't that strict, so anyone is capable of joining really.

Boop

This is our original first guild. The members in here are those who are capable of bossing, want to boss, or wish to eventually do so.

REQUIREMENTS

Level 120+

Attacker jobs: Night Lords, Shadowers, Bowmasters, Marksmen, Dark Knights, Heroes, Paladins, Buccaneers, and Corsairs. Mages: Bishops, Ice Lightning, and Fire Poisons.

HP Washed Attackers

Highly suggested/preferred.

Active

As long as there is some consistency, you should be fine. We understand everyone has their own lives, and things can get busy. If you are going to be away for quite some time, just let us know C:

Booper

This is our sister guild we are alliances with that was made only a week after Boop was created. In Booper, you can enjoy the same benefits as those in Boop while still being a more casual player if that is what choose to be.

REQUIREMENTS

Any Level, Any Job

Members here range from normally Lv50-100+, but we also accept those below that or higher than that.

Active

As long as there is some consistency, you should be fine. We understand everyone has their own lives, and things can get busy. If you are going to be away for quite some time, just let us know C:

Interested in our guild

We want players who can contribute to our community such as attending guild pictures, discussions, and events.
