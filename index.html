Join Us!

We are recruiting! We are looking for active, friendly members to join our guild. Depending on your level, class, you may be qualified to join one of our guilds. Guild members get exclusive prices for bossing services.

Boop

Boop is our main bossing guild, we are looking for high level characters who have hp wash.

REQUIREMENTS

Level 100+

Players must want to boss and be able to. Attacker jobs, such as Night Lords, BM/MM, warriors, Bucaneers, and Shadowers must be HP washed.

Active

This can mean being on every day, a couple times a week, or weekends only, as long as there is some consistency.

Interested in our guild

We want players who can contribute to our community such as attending guild pictures, discussions, and events.

Booper

Our sister guild who we are allianced with. In Booper you can enjoy the benefits as a casual player. Those interested in becoming an attacker can be moved up to Boop once you meet the level requirement.

REQUIREMENTS

Any Level, Any Job

We are mainly aiming for levels 50+ and jobs we are currently lacking.

Active

This can mean being on every day, a couple times a week, or weekends only, as long as there is some consistency.

Interested in our guild

We want players who can contribute to our community such as attending guild pictures, discussions, and events.
